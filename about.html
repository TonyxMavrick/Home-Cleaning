About CleanXpress

Your trusted cleaning partner, committed to delivering exceptional service with care, professionalism, and eco-friendly practices.

Our Story

Founded in 2021, CleanXpress was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves a clean, healthy home without the stress of doing it themselves. We started with a small team of passionate cleaners who believed in the power of a spotless space to transform lives.

Today, we've grown to serve over 1,200 satisfied customers across the USA. Our commitment to quality, eco-friendly practices, and exceptional customer service has made us a trusted name in home and commercial cleaning.

Every member of our team is carefully selected, thoroughly trained, and dedicated to exceeding your expectations. We don't just clean—we create spaces where you can live your best life.
